Overview

Purpose

The purpose of our website is to share with potential customers our main product that it is adventurous trips for first timers’ rafters, and pros to promote it and sell it. Our goal is to sell an experience to practice one of the most demanding sports there are to potential customers and regular clients, an in general adventurous people who just will like to test their limits and get closer to the most beautiful places in the world. Our company we will benefit from this website because we want people to find in, one place, information about our adventure’s trips, how to acquire it and how to book it. Our trips provide our potential customers and clients of a place where they can discover themselves and find a new meaning in their lives. We will show them that they do not need to worry about equipment, special clothing, food, and safety. Also, we will show them that we count with expert guides who will lead the way. Finally, the website will provide information about this sport, news, safety tips and will offer as well special.

Audience

We are aiming to college students from 21 to 30 years old who have or used to have an adventurous background and likes outdoors, can afford rafting experience, and want to spend a great summer time in a beautiful site. Also, we are targeting people who enjoyed camps and extreme sports on summer trips, like professionals within their 30´s, and middle age customers within their 40´s and 50´s who are financially independent and have enjoyed extreme sports and outdoors. Finally, we also want to bring to our site entrepreneurs in the field of self-development who are 30 to 45 years old and wants to test their limits and are looking for an experience to get rid of fears. My audience is typically tech savvy and uses computers and cellphones on daily basis.
